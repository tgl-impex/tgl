About Us

TGL Foreign Trade conducts import and export of steel products, steel sheets, food & pulses, and chemical products for partners in various industries.

With our international trade experience, strong supplier network, and customer-focused approach, we aim to offer quality products under competitive conditions.

At TGL Foreign Trade, establishing long-term partnerships and developing sustainable trade relations are among our core principles.

Our company actively trades with the MENA region, Europe, and Far East markets; carrying out commercial operations via sea and land transport.

From sourcing to shipment, we provide supportive services such as customs clearance, warehouse storage, and logistics coordination, offering end-to-end solutions to our clients.

We strictly comply with national and international regulations, ensuring that legal processes are executed thoroughly and meticulously.

Thanks to our dynamic structure, fast decision-making capability, and solution-oriented approach, we quickly adapt to changing market conditions, providing reliable and sustainable trade opportunities for our partners.
